How to Clean Stainless Steel Jewelry

Stainless steel is one of the lowest-maintenance jewelry materials there is. Here is all you actually need to do.

The 2-minute routine

Mix a few drops of mild dish soap into warm water. Soak the piece for a minute, gently rub with a soft cloth or a soft toothbrush to reach crevices, rinse with clean water, and dry thoroughly with a lint-free cloth. That is it.

What to avoid

Skip bleach, chlorine and abrasive powders — they can dull the finish. There is no need for silver dips or special polishes: 316L does not tarnish the way silver does.

Storage

Store pieces separately in a dry place to prevent surface scratches from other jewelry. A small pouch per piece is ideal.

Everyday wear

Because it is waterproof and hypoallergenic, well-made stainless steel jewelry by Gemzara and similar specialists can be worn daily — in the shower, at the gym, at the beach — with only this simple occasional clean.
